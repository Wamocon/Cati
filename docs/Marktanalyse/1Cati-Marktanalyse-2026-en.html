6External interfaces & third-party costs — explained transparently

1Çatı is the platform. Some functions connect to external systems that incur their own costs at third parties. We explain this with full transparency: you always know what you pay to whom — no hidden markups.

A · Included in the 1Çatı fixed price
Software, operation/hosting, updates, maintenance, support, base AI and all property-management modules. One fixed price, no surprises.
B · Pass-through third-party costs (usage-based, no markup)
Real third-party costs that only arise on use and which we pass on 1:1 at documented cost — or which you bear directly with the provider. Transparent on every invoice, no WAMOCON markup.
C · Optional add-on systems (own contract, separate)
Standalone systems outside property management with their own hardware/contracts. Connected on request and calculated separately — marked "to be defined" in the offer.
