## 1. Source Use Policy

This register records public sources used for planning and positioning. It is not a legal opinion, market-share ranking or vendor certification. Product, security and legal decisions should be validated with the client, selected vendors and qualified legal/accounting advisors before launch.

Public product, pricing, legal and security pages can change. Before final client approval, production launch, legal review or commercial claims, re-check all source links and record the review date here.

## 3. Evidence Handling Rules

- Do not present the Turkish competitor list as a legal market-share ranking.
- Use competitor claims only as public capability signals.
- Keep legal/compliance wording conservative.
- Confirm payment, bank, access, camera and meter vendor details before implementation.
- Update this register if new sources are used for pricing, compliance or vendor-specific integration decisions.
- Re-check pricing pages before procurement because public SaaS prices, metered limits and vendor quote terms can change.
