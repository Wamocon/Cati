2. The number management must budget

Total cost = recurring platform + usage + transaction fees + annual/prepaid fees + implementation/hardware + compliance/operations + contingency.

Known subtotal
Only priced, modelled lines. It is not the final budget while quotation items remain.

Keep currencies separate
USD SaaS, TRY local services, percentage-of-money fees and one-time cash must not be hidden inside one converted headline.

Largest variable
Payment and OTA/tour commissions can exceed hosting. Every 1% costs TRY 10,000 per TRY 1 million processed.

Quote required
Not zero means supplier/legal input is missing; it must stay visible outside the known subtotal.

Commercial claim conflict
The pitch page's €5/user/month and “zero development cost” are unapproved selling propositions, not total cost of ownership. Management must decide who absorbs every provider, transaction, hardware, compliance, onboarding and support line.

  • Supabase database, auth, realtime and PITR

3B. Turkish/local statutory

  • OTP/message packages and sender approvals
  • GİB/e-document credits and certificates
  • TTYS/EİDS, KEP, e-sign and domains
  • Package prepayment and expiry/renewal cash

3C. Usage & transactions

  • AI tokens, images, grounding and retries
  • WhatsApp, email, push, maps and identity checks
  • Image traffic, documents and retained storage
  • Payment, refund, chargeback and settlement fees
  • Channel manager, OTA and tour commissions

3D. One-time delivery

  • WP0–WP13 engineering, integration and UAT
  • Data cleansing, migration and reconciliation
  • Penetration test, load/race tests and restore drill
  • Provider onboarding, cutover and rollback

3E. Physical/site systems

  • Locks, barriers, readers, cards and installers
  • Cameras, VMS/NVR, meters and gateways
  • Cabling, SIM/network, licences and spares
  • Maintenance, inspections and replacement

3F. Governance & operations

  • Turkish legal, KVKK/SCC, MASAK and accountant
  • 24/7 support, on-call, incident response and audits
  • Training, hypercare, translation and content
  • Cyber insurance, tax, VAT, FX and contingency

4. Main cost drivers

  1. Money processed and commission rate
  2. Internal staff seats, support and SLA
  3. AI actions and context/document size
  4. SMS/WhatsApp and identity-check volume
  5. Images, maps, files and retention
  6. Sites, doors, cameras and field hardware

5. Activation waves

  1. Decide: entity, data location, merchant and inventory authority
  2. Foundation: production cloud, auth, storage, monitoring, restore
  3. Operate: payments, messaging, accounting, consent and bank matching
  4. Automate: PMS/OTA, KBS/ID, access, calendar and field work
  5. Scale: AI gateway, marketplace, tours and advanced providers

6. Decisions required now

  1. Define what “10,000 users” means and the required SLA.
  2. Approve cloud/data-transfer and Turkish compliance route.
  3. Select merchant-of-record, invoicing, refund and payout model.
  4. Issue comparable RFPs; separate public price, assumption and quote.
  5. Approve clean-DB, concurrency, backup/restore and UAT release gates.

Unpriced/quote-only examples: legal and accounting, KEP/WhatsApp, bank APIs, secure property-payment route, portals, smart access/camera hardware, data migration, security testing, support, training, tax and contingency.
